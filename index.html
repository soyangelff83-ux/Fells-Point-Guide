History of Baltimore

Baltimore, founded in 1729, grew as one of America’s most important port cities. Fells Point became a major shipbuilding center, famous for producing the fast “Baltimore Clippers.” The neighborhood played a key role in maritime trade, immigration, African American history, and the War of 1812.

Today, Fells Point blends historic charm with modern culture — cobblestone streets, waterfront views, music, food, and architecture that attract visitors from around the world.
