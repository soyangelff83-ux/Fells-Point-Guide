History of Baltimore

Baltimore, founded in 1729, became one of the most important port cities in the United States. Fells Point was a major shipbuilding center, famous for the fast “Baltimore Clippers” used in trade and the War of 1812.

The neighborhood is known for its cobblestone streets, maritime culture, African American history, and its role as a gateway for immigrants arriving by ship. Today, it blends historic charm with modern food, music, and waterfront views.

Local Tips & Helpful Advice

  • Stay on main streets: Thames St, Broadway, Aliceanna St.
  • Walk the waterfront: Best for photos and sunsets.
  • Use Uber/Lyft at night: Safe and reliable.
  • Try local food: Crab cakes and bakeries.
  • Avoid empty streets: Stay in well‑lit areas.
  • Everything is walkable: No car needed.
